# UX Test Script

## Introduction

    Hi! Thanks for helping me out. I’m testing an app called Focus Bear, which is meant to help people stay focused and productive. Just a reminder, I am testing the app, not you as a user. There are no right or wrong answers. Please try to think out loud as much as possible so I can understand your thought process. If you have any questions, feel free to ask. Do you have any questions before we get started?

## Scenario

    Imagine you are a student with a big exam coming up. You’ve just downloaded Focus Bear to help you stop procrastinating on social media. Your goal is to set up a 60-minute 'Deep Work' session to study for your exam. You also want to block distracting websites during your study sessions.
    Please go ahead and set up your first 'Deep Work' session using Focus Bear. Remember to think out loud and take your time. Let me know if you have any questions along the way.

## Tasks

    1. Open the Focus Bear app and navigate to the 'To-Do' or 'Focus Session' section.
    2. Set up a 'Deep Work' session for 60 minutes.
    3. Go to the 'Blocking' section and block distracting websites during your study sessions such as Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.
    4. Start your 'Deep Work' session and observe how the app helps you stay focused.
    5. Try to access one of the blocked websites during your session and see how the app responds.

## After the Tasks

    Thank you for completing the tasks! Now, I’d like to ask you a few questions about your experience using Focus Bear. Please feel free to share your honest thoughts and feedback.

    1. Can you walk me through your experience setting up that session? What was easy or difficult about it?
    2. How did the app's behavior affect your concentration?
    3. Did you encounter any issues or frustrations while using the app? If so, please describe them.
    4. If you had to describe your experience in three words, what would they be?
    5. What was confusing or unclear about the app, if anything?
    6. Was there anything you expected to happen that didn't?
    7. If you could change one thing about how the app works, what would it be and which part would it be?

    Thank you so much for your feedback! Your insights are really valuable and will help us improve Focus Bear. If you have any additional thoughts or suggestions, please feel free to share them now.
