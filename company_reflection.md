# Company Reflection: Focus Bear

## Reflection

**Why do you think Focus Bear was created?**

    Reading and exploring the Focus Bear website, I found that Focus Bear was created out of pure frustration and desperation. The founder, Jeremy, needed a way to manage his own AuDHD, and he realized that existing tools didn't do enough to stop the procrastination and the struggle of task initiation like when a plan for morning yoga accidentally turns into an hour of checking emails. He built Focus Bear as a way to obey his own best intentions rather than his distractions, creating a structured environment that guides users through their day and helps them stay on track.

**What problem is Focus Bear solving?**

    Focus Bear is solving the problem of losing control over ones's own time and intentions. It addresses the common issue of procrastination and difficulty with task initiation, especially for individuals with AuDHD. We all want to start our day with good intentions, but for others, there is a huge gap between wanting to do something and actually starting it. Focus Bear reduces the likelihood of getting sidetracked by distractions and helps users maintain focus on their goals by providing a structured environment that helps users stay on track with their intentions and manage their time effectively.

**Why do you think this mission is important?**

    This mission is important because the world is noisy and just full of distractions. Constant notifications and doom scrolling aren't just annoying, they actually hurt people’s mental health and self-esteem when they can’t get things done. Helping people actually follow through on their healthy habits makes a massive difference in how they feel about themselves at the end of the day.

**How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?**

    As someone into UI/UX and engineering, I’ve always preferred designs that actually help people rather than just looking pretty. I like the idea of using technology to solve a very human problem of distraction. It’s cool to work on something where the goal is to make the user’s life simpler and less stressful. It aligns with my interest in creating meaningful and impactful products that can genuinely improve people's lives, rather than just adding to the noise of the digital world.

**Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?**

    I personally relate to Focus Bear's mission since I'm also someone who struggles with procrastination and staying focused on my goals. Even when I started working on my onboarding process, I found myself getting distracted and losing focus, but the Focus Bear app helped me stay on track, maintain my focus on deep work, and manage my time well. So I can say that Focus bear has been really helpful for me as an intern and as an end-user.
