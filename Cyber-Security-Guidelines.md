# Cyber Security Guidelines

## Reflection

**What security measures do you currently follow, and where can you improve?**

    I currently use unique and long passwords for most accounts, especially my primary emails and accounts. I also made sure to have 2FA enabled on them. However, I can improve by going through all older accounts and moving them into a dedicated password manager so I would not use recycled passwords, eliminating the risk of a single breach compromising multiple platforms.

**How can you make secure behaviour a habit rather than an afterthought?**

    I will integrate a secure behavior into my routine or physical movement everyday by adding a "Security Check" as the mandatory first task in my Focus Bear startup. This ensures that verifying account security and environment safety becomes a standard part of my 8-hour workday. I will also practice the habit of manually locking my screen using Win + L every time I leave my workstation, regardless of whether I am working from home, at school, or any other location throughout this internship.

**What steps will you take to ensure your passwords and accounts are secure?**

    I will enable 2FA on every platform that supports it, specifically my emails and accounts that I use in major platforms that I will be using for work such as Figma and GitHub. I have already set my laptop and phone to auto-lock after 2 minutes of inactivity to protect my data in shared spaces. I will also add a monthly maintenance routine to periodically review authorized devices and active sessions to ensure no unauthorized access gets through.

**What would you do if you suspected a security breach or suspicious activity on your account?**

    I would immediately change my master password and revoke all active sessions to cut off unauthorized access. After securing the account, I would notify the Focus Bear team to alert them of the potential risk. Finally, I would check security logs to identify the specific cause and document a new habit to prevent a similar incident from happening again.
