## How I Work Best

- I do my best work when:
  - I have long, uninterrupted blocks of time for deep design work
  - I have a clear understanding of what I am working on and how to do it
  - I am well rested and well focused
  - I am in a peaceful and quiet environment/workplace

- I struggle when:
  - I am confused or unsure with what I'm doing
  - I get interrupted or distracted
  - The environment/workplace is very noisy

## Communication Style

- I prefer messages to be:
  - Direct: straight to the point
  - Factual: avoid vague descriptions, use specific terminology and clear examples
  - Batched: if you have multiple design tweaks, please send them in one comprehensive list rather than multiple separate messages

- The best way to reach me is:
  - If not urgent: please send an email (lableh.zapp@gmail.com)
  - If urgent: please message me on discord (@yogiyoink08)

## Collarboration & Teamwork

- I work best in a team when:
  - The team maintains an open dialogue: I value the opportunity to learn from the team's expertise
  - We can discuss the "why" behind design decisions
  - The environment is not tense
  - There is mutual respect

- My biggest pet peeve in teamwork is:
  - Ambiguity: vague feedback
  - There is less to no communication

- If I seem quiet in a meeting, it usually means:
  - I am listening intently and trying to understand everything.
  - I am too shy to speak or ask questions

## Focus & Productivity

- I stay focused by:
  - Working in a calm and quiet environment
  - Listening to calm music while working

- Things that distract me:
  - Loud noise
  - Calls and messages

- The best way to support my productivity is:
  - Provide clear and objective deadlines
  - Provide actionable and specific feedback

## Feedback & Growth

- I like feedback to be:
  - Direct and honest but kind
  - Ideally in private if it’s constructive
  - Specific with examples

- When I make a mistake, the best way to let me know is:
  - Point it out directly: I see mistakes as learning opportunities
  - Send me an email

- I appreciate it when teammates:
  - Listen and consider my suggestions
  - Suggest ideas and tips
